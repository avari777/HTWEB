Concert "100 Years of the Latvian National Opera" on 16th and 17th of November

On November 16 and 17 the Latvian National Opera troupe’s colourful centenary celebration concert 100 Years of the Latvian National Opera will feature the voices of the outstanding LNO soloists as well as internationally recognised and locally beloved opera stars - Marina Rebeka, Inga Kalna, Liudmyla Monastyrska, Dinara Alieva, Liene Kinča, Olesya Petrova, Zanda Švēde, Egils Siliņš, Stephen Gould, Ain Anger, Murat Karahan, Rame Lahaj, Irakli Kakhidze, Franco Vassallo, Vladislav Sulimsky. The concerts are being held as part of the Born in Latvia! (Dzimuši Latvijā!) concert cycle, which is being organized to celebrate Latvia's centenary and offers local music lovers to opportunity to hear many of Latvia's best singers and musicians.
